    What is Tornjak?

    Tornjak is a workload identity management plane built on top of SPIFFE/SPIRE to provide control over workloads spanning multiple clouds and platforms

    Global Visibility and Management Treating your workloads like “cattle, not pets” doesn’t mean that you lose sight of them… Manage and view your workload identities throughout your entire organization all in one place.

    Zero Trust and Compliance SPIRE provides a foundation of Zero Trust that organizations can leverage. Tornjak aims to provides a platform for policy, auditability and compliance for your organization’s workload identities.

    Based on Cloud Native Identity Standards Tornjak builds on top of the Cloud Native Computing Foundadtion (CNCF) SPIFFE/SPIRE projects' standard and implementation. Workload identities can be exposed through commonly used identity connectors like OIDC through SPIRE.

Take control of your workloads

Leverage tornjak and SPIFFE/SPIRE to create an organization wide multi-cloud workload identity based on Zero Trust.

Learn more Github

    What is Tornjak?

    Tornjak is a workload identity management built on top of SPIFFE/SPIRE to provide control over workloads spanning multiple clouds and platforms

    Global Visibility and Management Manage and view your workload identities throughout your entire organization.

    Based on Cloud Native Identity Standards Tornjak builds on top of the Cloud Native Computing Foundadtion (CNCF) SPIFFE/SPIRE projects' standard and implementation. Workload identities can be exposed through commonly used identity connectors like OIDC through SPIRE.

    Zero Trust and Compliance Tornjak uses SPIRE to provide a foundation of Zero Trust that organizations can leverage on, in addition to that, it provides a platform for policy, auditability and compliance for your organization’s workload identities.
